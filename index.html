ABOUT THE COLLECTIVE

Project 3- Reading response My first reading was ”The Web’s Grain” by Frank Chimero. One can explain the essay on the context of responsiveness but Chimero touched upon various outlooks in web design combined with philosophy and future approaches. He talks about how we must acknowledge and understand that The web is forcing our hands. And this is fine! Many sites will share design solutions, because we’re using the same materials. The consistencies establish best practices; they are proof of design patterns that play off of the needs of a common medium, and not evidence of a visual monoculture.
